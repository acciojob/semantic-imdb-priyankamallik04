Movie Summary

Interstellar is a science fiction movie directed by Christopher Nolan. The story follows a group of astronauts who travel through a wormhole in search of a new habitable planet for humanity as Earth faces environmental collapse. The movie explores themes of love, sacrifice, space exploration, and time.

Main Actors

Matthew McConaughey plays Cooper, a former NASA pilot. Anne Hathaway plays Amelia Brand, a scientist and astronaut. Jessica Chastain plays Murph, Cooper’s intelligent daughter.

Favorite Quote
